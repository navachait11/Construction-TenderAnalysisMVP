# Tender.AI — Customer Demo Talking Script
### Screen Recording Guide · Estimated Run Time: ~8–10 minutes

---

> **Before you hit record:**
> - Open `tender_analyst_demo.html` in Chrome (full screen, 1280px+ wide)
> - Dark mode should be the default — good, leave it there
> - Have a dummy PDF file on your Desktop ready to drag in (any PDF, even a blank one — the demo handles it automatically)
> - Mic on. Speak clearly and at a measured pace — this is a premium product.

---

## SCENE 1 — Opening Shot (0:00–0:30)
**What's on screen:** The homepage. Fraunces headline, upload zone, prompt chips.

**Click:** Nothing yet. Just let it breathe for 3–4 seconds.

**Say:**
> "This is Tender.AI — your AI-powered tender analyst built specifically for construction professionals. Instead of spending hours manually comparing quotes and reading through risk clauses, your team gets instant, structured intelligence across every project in your portfolio.
>
> What you're looking at is the workspace — it's clean by design. No clutter, no dashboards you'll never use. Just your projects, your documents, and the answers you need."

---

## SCENE 2 — Global Portfolio Toolbar (0:30–1:00)
**What's on screen:** Top toolbar — metric pills showing portfolio-level numbers.

**Mouse:** Slowly hover along the five metric pills (Total Projects → Portfolio Value → Avg Cost → Avg Prelims → Avg Duration).

**Say:**
> "Across the top you've got your live portfolio summary — always visible, no matter where you are in the app. Thirteen projects, fifty-four million dollars in portfolio value, average prelims tracking at eight-point-three percent.
>
> These numbers update automatically as new projects come in. You're never flying blind on where your portfolio sits."

---

## SCENE 3 — The Sidebar (1:00–1:45)
**What's on screen:** Left sidebar with 6 projects.

**Click:** Click the search bar, type "frank" — watch it filter to Frankston Hospital Wing B. Then clear it.

**Say:**
> "On the left, your project list. Think of it like your project inbox — all six active tenders in front of you, with their value and status at a glance. Active, Under Review, Draft — the system tracks where everything is.
>
> You can search across all your projects instantly. If you're managing twenty, thirty tenders, you can find anything in under a second."

**Click:** Click on **"Westfield Moorabbin Extension"** in the sidebar.

---

## SCENE 4 — Project Dashboard Strip (1:45–2:45)
**What's on screen:** Westfield project view — 4 metric cards + 4 insight cards.

**Mouse:** Gesture across the four metric cards.

**Say:**
> "The moment you open a project, the AI loads a full dashboard. Project value, program duration, prelims percentage, trade packages — all at the top.
>
> But here's where it gets interesting."

**Mouse:** Gesture across the four insight cards (Key Insight → Risk → Opportunity → Recommendation).

**Say:**
> "Below the metrics are four AI-generated intelligence cards. The system has already read through this project's documentation and flagged what matters.
>
> Key insight: potential one-hundred-and-forty thousand in concrete savings through subcontractor consolidation.
>
> Risk: the earthworks package has a six-week lead time that conflicts with the program start.
>
> Opportunity: joinery came back fourteen percent below budget — strong position for negotiation.
>
> And a direct recommendation: award structural steel to McNab Group. Two-hundred-and-twenty thousand below the next bidder, with no material exclusions.
>
> This is the kind of analysis that would normally take a senior estimator half a day. The AI does it before you've had your morning coffee."

---

## SCENE 5 — AI Chat Workspace (2:45–4:30)
**What's on screen:** Pre-loaded chat history below the dashboard.

**Mouse:** Scroll down slightly to show the chat thread.

**Say:**
> "Below the dashboard is the AI chat workspace. This is where your team actually works — asking specific questions about the project, comparing documents, stress-testing numbers.
>
> These conversations are pre-loaded here so you can see what a real session looks like."

**Mouse:** Point to the first user message — "Compare the two earthworks quotes."

**Say:**
> "Someone on the team asked: compare the two earthworks quotes and highlight any major differences. The AI came back immediately with a structured breakdown — key differences, risks, opportunities, and a clear recommendation: award to Terrain Group. The lower bid from Roberts Civil has a scope exclusion for rock breaking that'll likely come back as a variation worth up to a hundred and twenty thousand dollars."

**Mouse:** Point to the second exchange — the risk exposure table.

**Say:**
> "Then they asked about total risk exposure across the project. The AI returned a formatted table — three-forty to four-eighty thousand, broken into line items with specific causes. That's exactly what you'd put in a cost report, produced in under two seconds."

**Mouse:** Click in the chat input, type "Which package should we award first?" and press Enter.

**Wait** for the typing indicator and AI response.

**Say:**
> "And it's a live conversation. Ask anything — package comparisons, program risk, subcontractor suitability — and the AI responds instantly. Every message stays in the thread as you work."

## SCENE 8 — Document Upload Flow (5:45–7:30)
**What's on screen:** Homepage or any project view.

**Say:**
> "Now let me show you how a new tender document enters the system."

**Click:** Click the **Tender.AI logo** to return to the homepage.

**Say:**
> "From the homepage, your team can drop in a document directly. They don't need to set up a project first — just drag the file in."

**Action:** Drag a PDF file from your Desktop onto the upload zone (or anywhere on the screen).

**Say:**
> "I'm dragging in a tender document now—"

**On screen:** Body-level drag hint appears — golden border, "Drop to Analyse Document" label.

**Drop the file.**

**Say:**
> "Watch what happens."

**On screen:** Upload modal animates in — file icon bounces with particle burst, progress bar starts filling.

**Say:**
> "The document is uploading — you can see the progress in real time."

**On screen:** "Document Uploaded Successfully" status appears. Bar resets. "Analysing Document" begins.

**Say:**
> "Upload confirmed. Now the AI engine is processing the document — extracting trade packages, subcontractor data, risk flags, program details."

**On screen:** Analysis bar fills to 100%. Checkmark animates in. "Document Analysis Complete."

**Say:**
> "And we're done."

**On screen:** Modal closes. Sidebar animates — new project "Brighton Waterfront Precinct" appears, pulsing in gold. Project dashboard loads automatically.

**Say:**
> "The project is live. Brighton Waterfront Precinct — seven-point-two million, sixteen-month program. Notice the global portfolio metrics up top have updated automatically — thirteen projects now, fifty-four-point-five million in portfolio value."

**Mouse:** Gesture across the insight cards.

**Say:**
> "And the AI has already generated the full intelligence brief — key insight, risk, opportunity, recommendation. From document to analysis in under ten seconds."

## Tips for a Great Recording

- **Pace:** Slow down — let animations finish before you speak over them. Silence during the upload animation is fine; let it do the work.
- **Mouse movement:** Move deliberately and smoothly. Hover over things briefly before clicking.
- **Voice:** Measured, confident. This is a premium enterprise product — match the tone.
- **Screen size:** Record at 1440 × 900 or wider. The metric pills in the toolbar need room to breathe.
- **Retakes:** The upload animation is the hero moment. If it feels rushed, reset (refresh the page) and do that scene again.
- **Edit tip:** Cut 1–2 seconds of silence before the checkmark appears — keeps the pacing tight in the final edit.
